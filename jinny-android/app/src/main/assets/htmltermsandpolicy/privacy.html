The privacy and security of personal data is one of our top priorities. Should you have any queries, concerns, or feedback, please feel free to contact us at hello@myjinny.com.


1. Personal Data Protection Policy

This document will outline how Jinny collects, uses, and protects your personal data. “Personal Data”, in this document, refers to any data about an individual who can be identified from that data, or together with other information to which we have access to, including but not limited to, data in our existing records. We conduct our business in compliance with the Personal Data Protection Act (No. 26 of 2012) (“The Act”). Subject to your rights at law, you agree to be bound by the prevailing terms of our Personal Data Protection Policy as updated from time to time on Jinny Platforms.


2. Personal Data Collection

The Personal Data that Jinny may collect from you depends on the Services you use. Under typical use of the Services, the Personal Data relating to you that we may collect are:

(a) Device ID and e-mail address; during app installation and registration of account.

(b) Month and year of birth, gender, residential region; optional input by user in their profile.

(c) Photos selected by user, requiring camera access; during submission of photo proof of receipts, required to be eligible for cashback.

(d) Purchase information as provided on submitted receipts (e.g. merchant and location of purchase, time of purchase, items of purchase, amount of purchase); during submission of photo proof of receipts, required to be eligible for cashback.

(e) Payment-related information (e.g. bank account number, full name of bank account holder); provided by user during processing of cashback redemption via bank transfer.

(f) Mailing address and name of recipient; provided by user during processing of cashback redemption via purchase of physical vouchers.

(g) Membership and associated membership number; provided by user when storing membership information.

(h) Preferences; provided by user in settings, user actions on promotions/vouchers, user interactions on the Jinny Platforms, feedback or comments provided by user.

In addition to collecting the above Personal Data directly from you through the Jinny Platforms, Jinny may also collect Personal Data from third parties, including but not limited to, our partners, fraud-prevention agencies, social media providers, or from publicly available sources.


3. Accuracy of Information You should ensure that all Personal Data you provide us is accurate, complete, true, and correct. Failure to do so may prevent or limit our ability to provide you with our Services.

You can update your personal information anytime by accessing your Jinny account. In the event that you are unable to update your personal information through your Jinny account, you may contact us at hello@myjinny.com, and we will assist you as best as we can.

In the event when you provide us with any Personal Data belonging or associated with a third party, including but not limited to your family members or employees, you represent to us that you have obtained their consent to provide us with their Personal Data.


6. Personal Data Security

Jinny places a high priority to ensure that all Personal Data collected will be safely and securely stored. Measures in place include:

(a) Personal Data relating to you is stored on servers with proper safeguards to prevent security breaches.

(b) Personal Data relating to you during transmission and storage on Jinny Platforms are safely secure in accordance to the authentication and security standards of the applicable operating systems.

(c) Access of information relating to Personal Data on our systems is only limited to authorized personnel.

(d) Strict verification process is implemented to prevent unauthorized access to Personal Data.

(e) Source code relating to our business are properly secured and/or privately hosted to prevent vulnerabilities exposure.

(f) Personal Data that are no longer needed for our business and/or legal processes will be securely destroyed.

Jinny does not sell or trade Personal Data we collected with third parties.

(a) Providing the Services to you. This includes, without limitation, using Personal Data relating to you to track and monitor your transactions with merchants/partners, credit/debit cashback to your Jinny account, process the necessary cashback redemption via bank transfer and/or voucher purchase, storing and displaying your membership information, provide you with promotions and/or offers, provide the Services in accordance with your preferences, and administer promotional events, contests, and/or corporate social responsibility projects.

(c) Providing customer service to you. This includes, without limitation, using Personal Data relating to you to respond to your enquiries, requests, or other issues, provide you with assistance and/or updates relating to our Services or policies.

(d) Ensuring compliance and security. This includes, without limitation, using Personal Data relating to you to detect and prevent fraud and/or any other crimes, conduct internal audits, meet legal, regulatory, and/or other requirements, manage security and risks concerns, and ensure the safety and security of our properties, systems, Jinny Platforms, and the Services we provide.

Jinny will not use Personal Data relating to you for purposes other than what we have informed you, or which we are permitted and/or required under applicable law and regulations.

Jinny will only store Personal Data relating to you for the duration where there is a business or legal need to do so.
